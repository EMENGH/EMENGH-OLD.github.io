Jeopardy Game!

this game consists of one main board containing a statement and 4 possible options to match the original statement. The game begins when a player selects a category and a price cell (button) on the screen. player is presented at that point with a statement and 4 possible matches in the form of a question. when player selects one of the possible matches, the game display a message stating the selection was correct or incorrect. if the selection was correct then the price is then added to an accummulator. otherwise it will be deducted from the total. The game ends when all the buttons have been selected. Player can click on the reset/restart button at anytime to initiate another game. The variation in prices on the board corresponds to the difficulty of the question..
